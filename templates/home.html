Core Features

Everything your health center needs

Designed for nurses, doctors, and administrators to coordinate care efficiently and securely.

🧑‍⚕️
Patient Registration
Quick and structured intake forms to register walk-in patients with complete demographic and clinical details.
🚨
Triage Prioritization
Assign and track Critical, High, Medium, and Low priority levels with real-time visual indicators.
📋
Patient Queue
Live queue dashboard for doctors showing patients sorted by priority and waiting time.
📊
Analytics & Reports
Daily and weekly reports on patient volume, priority distribution, and department performance.
🔐
Role-Based Access
Separate views and permissions for Nurses, Doctors, and Administrators to maintain data security.
📡
REST API
Public statistics API endpoint for integration with external health monitoring dashboards.
How it works

Patient journey at a glance

1
Patient Arrives
Nurse registers the patient and records presenting complaints.
2
Triage Assessment
Nurse assigns a priority level and logs vital signs.
3
Doctor Review
Doctor picks up from the prioritized queue and starts consultation.
4
Consultation Logged
Diagnosis, treatment, and status are recorded for audit and reporting.
